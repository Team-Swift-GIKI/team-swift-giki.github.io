About Us

Team Swift, a student-led team from the Ghulam Ishaq Khan Institute, specializes in the design and production of cutting-edge Unmanned Aerial Systems. This year, the team is set to make history as one of the first-ever Pakistani representatives at the prestigious AUVSI SUAS Competition in the United States, organized by the world's largest non-profit organization devoted to advancing unmanned systems and robotics.

This exciting opportunity not only puts Pakistan on the map for technological innovation, but it also provides our students with a unique chance to expand their knowledge and skills in the rapidly growing field of UAVs.

Moreover, we are delighted to offer our services to partner with any organization interested in developing delivery drones, as our team is fully equipped and proficient in UAV design and development.

Our Projects

Bumblebee - Team's first quadcopter, capable of carrying up to 500 grams payload and also flying banners for marketing campaigns. It can be seen pictured after completing 8 successful flights.

Shaheen - A radio-controlled cargo aircraft capable of aerial deployment.

Baby Shark - This little trainer made out of depron sheets was the first aircraft fabricated by Team Swift.

Snapper - Built for a gentler introduction to radio control flying to new team members. RC-powered gliders are a great option for learning to fly.

Flamingo - Designed and fabricated from scratch, entirely by the women engineers at Team Swift. With its semi-symmetric airfoil, flamingo is ideal for aerobatics.

Hydra - Capable of fully autonomous aerial drops. With its 17-inch carbon fiber propellers, Hydra can carry up to 1kg payload for over 15km.

Recluse - Our trusty UAV named for its extremely stable flying and unmatched payload delivering capacity.
